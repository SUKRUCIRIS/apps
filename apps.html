Foreign Stock Tax Calculator for Turkish Citizens

A user-friendly application that enables Turkish citizens to calculate taxes on their earnings from foreign stock markets. The app allows data input through a graphical interface, CSV files, or account statements from IBKR (Interactive Brokers). Based on the provided data, it automatically generates a detailed tax report, which can be exported as a PDF. The app is currently available only in Turkish. The application requires a one-time purchase and grants lifetime access. All future updates are available free of charge.
